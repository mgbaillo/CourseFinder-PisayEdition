Website's Purpose

Students who graduate from the Philippine Science High School must enroll in a bachelor's degree program in science. This is a mandate based on the Scholarship Agreement Contract. This website aims to assist students in selecting a course from undergraduate courses approved by the Philippine Science High School System. Students can choose what Science and Technology course to take related to the field of Biology, Chemistry or Engineering/Mathematics. Those students who plan to be a DOST Undergraduate scholar can also see the approved Undergraduate course by the DOST-SEI. A list of the approved courses that are offered is given under this website.
